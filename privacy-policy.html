Notes V Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Overview

Notes V is a private notes, checklist, image, drawing, voice recording, meeting recording, transcription, summarization, translation, and reminder app.

Encrypted Notes By Default

Your note content is encrypted by default before it is stored in Firebase. This includes note titles, body text, checklist text, image and drawing references, audio references, colors, and related note content. The goal is that private note data is not readable directly from the database.

Account Information

When you create an account or continue with Google, Firebase Authentication may process your email address, user ID, authentication provider, and sign-in metadata. Notes V uses this information to sign you in and sync your notes to your own account.

Files, Audio, Images, And Drawings

If you add images, drawings, or recordings, the app may store encrypted file data or encrypted references in Firebase Storage and Firestore. Meeting recordings may also be saved on your device so you can retry summarization later or delete them when you choose.

AI And Offline Processing

Notes V is designed to use free and local tools where possible, including on-device or bundled processing for speech and summaries. If an optional online service is configured by the app owner in the future, only the required text or audio for that feature should be sent for processing.

Reminders And Notifications

If you set a reminder, the app schedules a local device notification. Reminder notifications are handled by your device operating system and may include the note title or a short note preview.

What We Do Not Sell

Notes V does not sell your personal notes, images, drawings, recordings, summaries, or account information.

Your Choices

You can edit or delete notes, delete saved recordings, remove reminders, and sign out. Deleting a note removes the note record and the app attempts to remove related stored files.

Security Limits

Encryption improves privacy, but no app, device, cloud service, or network can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure. Keep your device, password, and Google account protected.

Contact

For privacy questions, support, or data requests, contact the app owner using the support email listed on the Play Store page.
